Nairobi - The Forger

Nairobi, portrayed by Alba Flores, brings an unparalleled skill set to the Royal Mint of Spain heist in "Money Heist." As the master forger, her precision and artistic flair play a crucial role in the success of the plan. Nairobi's character is a testament to resilience, warmth, and leadership, making her a beloved figure among fans. Her emotional depth, coupled with a compassionate spirit, adds a unique dynamic to the series, creating a character who is both formidable in her abilities and empathetic in her interactions. Nairobi's journey unfolds as a poignant exploration of strength and vulnerability within the intricate world of Money Heist.
