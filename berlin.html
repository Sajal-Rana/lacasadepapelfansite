Berlin - The Mastermind

Berlin, brilliantly portrayed by Pedro Alonso, stands out as the mastermind behind the Royal Mint of Spain heist in "Money Heist." His strategic intellect, charismatic leadership, and enigmatic personality make him a central figure in the gripping storyline. Berlin's complex character, filled with both ruthlessness and vulnerability, adds layers to the narrative, leaving viewers intrigued by his actions and decisions. As the orchestrator of the heist, Berlin's presence leaves an indelible mark on the series, showcasing the intricate balance between brilliance and internal struggles within the world of Money Heist.
