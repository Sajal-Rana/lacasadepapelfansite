Moscow - The Mint Supervisor

Moscow, portrayed by Paco Tous, assumes the role of the Mint Supervisor in the Royal Mint of Spain heist in "Money Heist." With a background in minting currency, Moscow brings both expertise and a fatherly presence to the gang. His character is defined by a sense of responsibility, loyalty, and a desire to provide for his son Denver. Moscow's tragic journey unfolds amidst the complexities of the heist, showcasing the human side of the characters involved. As one of the emotional pillars of the series, Moscow's story explores themes of family, sacrifice, and the consequences of the criminal world.
