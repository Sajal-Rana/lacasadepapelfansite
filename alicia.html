Alicia Sierra - The Determined Investigator

Alicia Sierra, portrayed by Najwa Nimri, is a determined investigator in "Money Heist." Initially working for the Spanish National Police, Sierra becomes a formidable opponent for the Professor and the gang. Her relentless pursuit of justice and unyielding nature make her a force to be reckoned with. Sierra's character is marked by intelligence, cunning, and a strong sense of duty. As the series progresses, Alicia Sierra's personal and professional life becomes entangled with the complex events surrounding the heists, adding layers to her character. Sierra's presence introduces a formidable adversary, contributing to the high-stakes tension within the world of "Money Heist."
